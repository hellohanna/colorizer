Upload your dataset and train a model

This is a page for creating your own colorizing model. This app uses dataset to train a custom image-to-image translation model. By default it's trained to colorise portrait photos. To add additional process button you should first create your own dataset. The first step to do this is to choose a topic of pictures you want this app to colorize. It can be nature, cars, houses etc. The result of the trained model depends on how big provided dataset is. To provide the dataset pack all photos to zip archive and upload.
